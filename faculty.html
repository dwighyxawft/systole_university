Discover the world of UC Berkeley

College of Letters & Science

The largest college at UC Berkeley, the College of Letters and Science is home to 40+ departments and programs.

College of Engineering

The College of Engineering is one of the best engineering schools in the country. It is known for its cutting-edge research and academic excellence.

College of Environmental Design

The College of Environmental Design offers a wide range of programs in architecture, urban planning, landscape architecture, and environmental design.

College of Chemistry

The College of Chemistry is one of the leading chemistry schools in the world. It is known for its innovative research and top-notch faculty.

Haas School of Business

The Haas School of Business is one of the top business schools in the country. It is known for its rigorous curriculum and strong focus on entrepreneurship and innovation.

College of Natural Resources

The College of Natural Resources offers a diverse array of programs in environmental science, conservation, and resource management.

College of Education

The College of Education is dedicated to training the next generation of educators and leaders in the field of education.

College of Art

The College of Art offers a wide range of programs in visual arts, performing arts, and design. It is known for its vibrant creative community and innovative interdisciplinary approach.

College of Information

The College of Information is at the forefront of the digital revolution, offering cutting-edge programs in data science, human-computer interaction, and information management.
